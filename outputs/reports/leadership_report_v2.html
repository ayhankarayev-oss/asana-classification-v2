New in v2

  • NEW Issue Type: "Capital & Cash Flow Management" (Cash Flow + Commitment = 221 docs)
  • STANDALONE: "Ownership & Trust Structure" (139 docs) — its own Issue Type now
  • EXPANDED: "Portfolio & Investment Operations" now includes Debt & merged Security/Position (560 docs)
Rationale: (1) Security updates and position cleanup are the same operational action — modifying how an instrument is represented. (2) Capital calls and cash distributions are two sides of the same fund cash lifecycle. (3) Debt/lending are financial instruments managed as portfolio positions. (4) Ownership/trust is a distinct legal-structural domain separate from cash flows.
